Child Safety Standards

Date Jugaad | Last updated: July 2026

Our Commitment

Date Jugaad is committed to maintaining a safe platform and has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. We do not allow any content, behavior, or activity that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors.

Age Requirement

Date Jugaad is strictly for users aged 18 and above. Users must confirm they are 18+ during registration. Any user found to be under 18 will have their account permanently removed.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Any content that sexualizes minors in any way
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Any attempt to contact minors through our platform

Enforcement

We take the following actions to enforce these standards:

  • Immediate and permanent account termination for any violation
  • Reporting confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant Indian authorities
  • Full cooperation with law enforcement investigations
  • User reporting tools available in-app to flag suspicious behavior

Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates these standards, please report it immediately via the in-app report button or by emailing us at support@datejugaad.com.

Contact

For any questions about our child safety standards, contact us at datejugaad@gmail.com.
